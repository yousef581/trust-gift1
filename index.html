I'm not here to tie you down

I just want your trust… and your comfort when I'm around.

To Manar

Manar...
There are feelings we carry that words often fail to express.
But your presence in my life has always meant a lot...
Even if we don’t talk all the time,
I see you as a dear sister.
All I wish for is to earn your trust,
and always be the one you can talk to —
no judgment, no pressure…
just a heart that listens,
respects you, understands you,
and stands by you without needing to be asked.

Gifted with care – 19 July 2025
